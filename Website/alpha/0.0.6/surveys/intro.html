Overview

We collected some data on people’s thoughts on quantum technologies: attendants at the Festival of Nature in Bristol; researchers in the Bristol Centre for Quantum Photonics; and we polled over 2000 students, lecturers and University staff.

The UK government has invested £270 million in quantum technologies and the European union plans to invest €1 billion, clearly the prevailing belief is that these technologies will open new markets, improve our lives and push the boundaries of science even further.

So we wondered: What do people inside and outside the industry know and think about these technologies? Here are some of the results.
